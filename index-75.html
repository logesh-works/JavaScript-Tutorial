Edit commands more easily

Writing code in your terminal shouldn’t feel like 1978. Warp’s modern text input editor is more like a modern IDE with selections, cursor positioning, and completion menus.

Navigate faster with less scrolling

Move back and forth through your history, command by command, instead of scrolling through walls of text. Copy output with one click.

Form commands at the speed of thought

Run commands with the right parameters

Save your most commonly used workflows and execute them on-demand. Share workflows with your team and update them for everyone in real time.

Use Warp AI to look up commands on the fly

Powered with OpenAI APIs, Warp AI can convert natural language into executable shell commands. Ask Warp AI to debug errors, write scripts, or walk you through next steps.

Make Warp your own

Select from popular themes or create your own.
