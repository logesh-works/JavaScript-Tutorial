TPAC 2023 will be a hybrid meeting in Sevilla, Spain, 11-15 September.

  • Conference registration: https://www.w3.org/register/tpac2023
  • Hotel registration: https://www.w3.org/2023/09/TPAC/venue.html (use button "Book your guestroom")
  • Registrants: https://www.w3.org/register/tpac2023/registrants (Member-only link)
