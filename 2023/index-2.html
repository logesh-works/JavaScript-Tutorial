Latest Updates – 24 July 2023

We hope you enjoyed WordCamp Europe 2023 and have a safe journey home! Initial details of our 2024 event in Torino, Italy will be provided over the next few weeks.

This week we’ll be updating this website with our closing posts, including some interesting stats and financial details – before handing over to our 2024 website. If you haven’t already, you can signup for our WCEU2024 updates via newsletter here.

All talks and workshops which have been recorded during the event, will be available on WordPress.tv shortly, and our amazing photography team are also working through the thousands of photos captured and these will be available on our Flickr account once ready.

Have feedback? We’ve now launched our WordCamp Europe 2023 Post-Event Survey – we’d love to hear your feedback to help our teams plan for our next event. If you have feedback on our event schedule, you can provide feedback on any sessions (such as talks or workshops) which you attended.

A World-Class Location

Our amazing venue, the Megaron Athens International Conference Centre is located in the centre of Athens, just a short walk away from a range of historic landmarks and is one of the largest venues in the capital, hosting 6,000 people with a range of meeting and auditoriums.
