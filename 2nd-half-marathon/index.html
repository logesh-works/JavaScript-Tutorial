City Half Marathon

Sunday, July 21st, 2024

Start Time: 8:15 a.m.
Start Line: Presidio, 610 Old Mason St. (see shuttle info below)
Finish Line: Embarcadero at Folsom (Full Marathon finish line)
Distance: 13.1 miles with a 3.5-hour time limit.
Race Fee: $151

The 2nd Half Marathon course is USATF certified. What Youll Experience: The 2nd Half City Marathon is a local favorite as it winds through iconic city neighborhoods and finishes at the official San Francisco Marathon Finish Lineand with a later start time and faster route, runners have more time to take in the festivities! This unique and memorable route is packed with experiences, including:

  • Crissy Field with the Golden Gate Bridge so close you can almost touch it.
  • Refreshing Bay breezes with Eucalyptus in the air – a constant while running marathons in San Francisco.
  • Picturesque views of the Sea Cliff neighborhood with the Pacific Ocean as the backdrop.
  • Golden Gate park and the iconic Haight Street.
  • Historic Mission District and the newly developed Mission Bay District.
  • Up-close views of major league sports stadiums including homes of the Golden State Warriors and the San Francisco Giants.
  • Positive energy from spectators at the finish line and along the Bay Bridge.

What You Need to Know: Runner Shuttle Buses will pick up runners from 6:45 am to 7:30 am in 5 to 10 min. intervals from the Main Start Line near the Embarcadero, at Howard St. and Spear St., and drop off at the start line of the City Half Marathon, at Halleck and Mason St, in the Presidio.

2nd Half Marathon Details

  • Start time, location and corral details
    Start Time: 8:15am*, in the Presidio, 610 Old Mason Street @ Sports Basement All runners need to be in their corral at the start of the race. Corral time and locations are subject to change.

    Corral Start Times:

    Corral A Corral B Corral C
    8:15 AM 8:24 8:33
    8:18 AM 8:27 8:36
    8:21 AM 8:30 8:39 AM

    For more information on starting corrals, please clickhere

  • Course Limits
    In order to qualify as an Official 2nd Half Marathon Finisher, you must finish within the 3.5 hour course limit.
  • Water/Fuel Stops & Medical Care
    The 2nd Half Marathon includes 10 water stops where you can find water, Nuun electrolytes, medical, and toilets. Water Stop 5 includes Chargel energy gel.

    *All water stops will have water monster refill stations.

  • Need to know
    In an effort to reduce waste and work toward being a sustainable event, heat sheetswill not be provided at the finish line. Our medical team will have them available if the weather is cold and foggy. We encourage you to pack a warm lightweight item that can fit in your drop bag that you can put on post-race. We appreciate your understanding and help in reducing our footprint.
  • Race Morning Runner Shuttles
    Runner Shuttle Buses will pick up runners from Howard St. and Spear St. by the Main Start Line near the Embarcadero from 6:45 am to 7:30 am in 5 to 10-minute intervals and drop off by the 2nd Half Marathon start line, at Halleck and French Ct, in the presidio.
  • Drop-off Points
    If you are getting a ride in on race day, the best drop-off point is Lincoln Blvd. between Montgomery St and Anza Ave, in The Presidio. Pick up for the 2nd Half MarathonFinish: Mission at Main St. orWashington St. at Drum St.
  • Awards
    The top 3 overall and the top 3 Men, Women, and NB+ division winners in the 2nd Half marathon will receive digital 3D awards via email when the results are finalized, 3 to 6 weeks post-race.
  • Divisions
    Open, Masters and Age Groups: 19 and Under, 20-24, 25-29, 30-34, 35-39, 40-44, 45-49, 50-54, 55-59, 60-64, 65-69, 70-74, 75-79, 80+
