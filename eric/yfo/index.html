Your Father's Oldsmobile

Music from the period 1920 - 1950, roughly speaking.
Wednesday mornings from 8:00am until 10:00am, Eastern time.

Sadly, I was forced to discontinue the show in fall 2005 due to time constraints. The description that follows is included mainly for historical reasons.

Finally, a reason to look forward to the morning drive time, and a great way to propel you into the second half of the week! Start your Wednesday off right with swing, jazz, and other favorites starring the likes of Glenn Miller, Woody Herman, Duke Ellington, Louis Jordan, Louis Armstrong, Sidney Bechet, Muggsy Spanier, Kid Ory, the International Sweethearts of Rhythm, Clarence Williams, Billie Holiday, Benny Goodman, Bix Beiderbecke, Alberta Hunter, Jabbo Smith, The Andrews Sisters, Artie Shaw, Charlie Barnet, King Oliver, Ella Fitzgerald, Chick Webb, and so much more. Requests and dedications are always welcomed.
