  • Gravimetric display
  • You can use the arrow keys to increment/decrement values ±1 (shift-arrow ±10; control-arrow ±0.1).
  • Unlike most ‘artillery’ games, here both ships fire simultaneously, so get all the values entered before you hit “Fire!”.
  • Shots become “live” after about a quarter-second, meaning your own shot can kill you.
  • Shots that are still in flight after about half a minute will self-destruct.
  • The “GAME OVER” screen lasts five seconds. Then you’re thrown back into battle. Neverending battle.
  • If you get a boring game layout, which happens, just reload the page.
