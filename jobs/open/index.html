We welcome applicants regardless of age, disability, gender, race, marital status, religion, sexual orientation, or whatever type of systemic discrimination they have faced. Open Positions

  • Browsers Developer (Chromium focus)

    At Igalia we are looking for a new developer to contribute to both the Chromium web browser and the Blink layout engine.

  • Browsers Developer (WebKit focus)

    At Igalia, we are looking to hire a developer for our browsers team, whose task will be to maintain and improve WebKit, the well-known web engine used by millions of people.

  • Compilers Team Developer

    Igalia is looking to hire a developer for its compilers team. Our team works on a number of programming language technologies including JavaScript, WebAssembly, and LLVM.

  • Core Engineer

    At Igalia, we are looking to hire an engineer for our Core Team.

  • GStreamer Developer

    We are looking for developers to work on GStreamer, as both a user of the API, and a contributor to the internals of the framework.

  • Graphics Developer

    At Igalia, we have positions open on our graphics team, which has a particular focus on Linux graphics.

  • Kernel Developer

    At Igalia, we are looking to hire a developer to contribute to the Linux kernel.

  • Multimedia Developer

    We are looking to hire someone to join our multimedia team.

  • Web Platform Engineer

    Igalia is looking for someone to join our web platform team.

Don’t fit any of these roles but interested in joining us?
